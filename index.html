Rosa SanMarchi is a Chicago based actor. She's a member of SAG-AFTRA and is represented by Grossman & Jack Talent.

Rosa began her acting career in her hometown of Phoenix, AZ. She spent several years in Los Angeles where she studied at the Carter Thor Studio. During her time in LA she acted in, directed, wrote and produced numerous on-camera and theatrical projects.

Rosa is now proud to be a Chicago actor working in film, TV and theater.

This summer, Rosa is attending the 2015 School at Steppenwolf.
